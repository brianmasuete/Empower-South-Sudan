Services

Empower South Sudan works to address the complex challenges and factors that drive social and economic vulnerabilities using locally led multidisciplinary, integrated solutions. Our key thematic areas of focus include health, nutrition, livelihoods and economic development, risk communication and social behavior change, education, gender, and WASH.

OUR CORE THEMATIC AREAS OF FOCUS

We use the most efficient local solutions that provide optimum results, in local contexts. Our core program services include:

HEALTH

At Empower South Sudan, we focus on improving health outcomes for youth, women, and children through comprehensive healthcare services. Our programs include primary healthcare, HIV/AIDS prevention and treatment, family planning, and reproductive health services. We work closely with the Ministry of Health to address emerging health challenges and implement innovative digital health solutions.

LIVELIHOODS AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

Our economic development programs empower individuals to build sustainable livelihoods through employment opportunities, entrepreneurship, and skills training. We focus on financial inclusion, agricultural development, and supporting marginalized groups to achieve economic independence and community development.

GENDER, EQUITY, PROTECTION AND SOCIAL INCLUSION

We address gender inequality and gender-based violence through comprehensive programs that support women, youth, and vulnerable groups. Our initiatives focus on risk mitigation, response, and prevention strategies, particularly for adolescent girls and young women living with HIV.

NUTRITION

We implement nutrition programs that ensure healthy growth and development, with special focus on pregnant women, breastfeeding mothers, and children. Our strategies prevent malnutrition and strengthen local nutrition security in both development and emergency contexts.

DATA ANALYSIS

Our strategic information unit provides comprehensive data management and analysis services. We focus on community-led monitoring and advocacy research to ensure evidence-based decision making and program effectiveness.

EMPOWER SOUTH SUDAN

Empower South Sudan is a national, Youth-led non-profit, non governmental organization founded in 2018 with the goal to improve health, education, and economic opportunities for vulnerable youth, adolescent girls and young women, their families in South Sudan through provision of integrated health, education, nutrition, GBV, WASH, Livelihoods and Economic empowerment programs.

Mission and Values
  • To advocate for the fundamental human rights and improve the health and socio-economic status of adolescents, young people and their families through provision of integrated innovative solutions, self empowerment, education, health, nutrition, WASH, and livelihood enhancement services
Our core values
  • Equity: we believe that all people regardless of age, sex, religion, and social class have equal right to access basic social services to achieve their full potential.
  • Mutual Respect: We believe and respect the dignity and the potential of all people. .
  • Transparency and Accountability:We recognize the need to maintain the highest level of integrity and honesty in all our actions. We remain committed to ensuring proper accountability to our development partners and other stakeholders. .
  • Protection of human dignity and the rights:Of vulnerable women and adolescent girls and other marginalized groups..
Our Vision
  • is an empowered and healthy adolescents and young people in South Sudan.
