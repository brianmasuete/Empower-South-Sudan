Services

Empower South Sudan works to address the complex challenges and factors that drive social and economic vulnerabilities using locally led multidisciplinary, integrated solutions. Our key thematic areas of focus include health, nutrition, livelihoods and economic development, risk communication and social behavior change, education, gender, and WASH.

OUR CORE THEMATIC AREAS OF FOCUS

We use the most efficient local solutions that provide optimum results, in local contexts. Our core program services include:

HEALTH

  • At Empower South Sudan, improving the health of the youth and vulnerable adolescent girls and young women, men and children is core to our mission. We believe that a good health for a household provides the foundation for community economic empowerment and development. We aspire to deliver and provide basic primary, secondary and tertiary healthcare, comprehensive HIV/AIDS prevention, care and treatment, family planning and sexual reproductive health services using our innovative approaches tailored to local contexts and what people need to be healthy and to generate the evidence needed to address their health challenges.
  • Empower SS work with Ministry of Health and other stakeholders to addresses emerging pandemics and epidemics with local expertise and supports State and County Health Departments in improving epidemic preparedness, detection, and response. Our local approaches incorporate digital health innovations and use cutting-edge behavioral, epidemiological, and existing clinical research sciences to understand, prevent and contain these emerging threats.
    Our cross-cutting capabilities in healthcare and control of pandemic threats include:
    • Focused primary healthcare services
    • HIV/AIDS prevention, care & treatment services
    • Family planning and sexual reproductive health services
    • Social behavior change, risk communications and social marketing
    • Food security and emergency nutrition
    • Water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH)
    • Community-led monitoring.

LIVELIHOODS AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

Empower’ s Livelihoods and economic development programs are designed to help individuals create and achieve sound livelihoods and economic future for themselves and their families. Our support focuses on increasing participants’ productivity, employment, and household incomes through farming, cash transfers, skills training, entrepreneurship, and other socio economic activities. Our cross-cutting capabilities in livelihood and economic strengthening include:

  • Employment and Entrepreneurship
  • Financial inclusion
  • Livelihoods and economic strengthening
  • Agriculture and food systems development
  • Livelihoods and PLHIV and other marginalized groups

GENDER, EQUITY, PROTECTION AND SOCIAL INCLUSION

Empower SS understand that in South Sudan, adolescent girls, women and youths are disproportionately affected and are at higher risk of experiencing gender- based violence. Therefore, our gender, equity, protection, and social inclusion programs help, and support individuals and communities to prioritize and address both gender inequality issues and GBV, specifically among AGYW and children living with HIV with programmatic focus on GBV risk mitigation, response and prevention.

NUTRITION

Good nutrition is essential for healthy growth and development, particularly for pregnant and breastfeeding women and their children. Empower SS designs and implement program strategies that prevent and treat malnutrition and strengthen local nutrition security in development and emergency contexts.

EMPOWER SOUTH SUDAN

Empower South Sudan is a national, Youth-led non-profit, non governmental organization founded in 2018 with the goal to improve health, education, and economic opportunities for vulnerable youth, adolescent girls and young women, their families in South Sudan through provision of integrated health, education, nutrition, GBV, WASH, Livelihoods and Economic empowerment programs.

Mission and Values
  • To advocate for the fundamental human rights and improve the health and socio-economic status of adolescents, young people and their families through provision of integrated innovative solutions, self empowerment, education, health, nutrition, WASH, and livelihood enhancement services
Our core values
  • Equity: we believe that all people regardless of age, sex, religion, and social class have equal right to access basic social services to achieve their full potential.
  • Mutual Respect: We believe and respect the dignity and the potential of all people. .
  • Transparency and Accountability:We recognize the need to maintain the highest level of integrity and honesty in all our actions. We remain committed to ensuring proper accountability to our development partners and other stakeholders. .
  • Protection of human dignity and the rights:Of vulnerable women and adolescent girls and other marginalized groups..
